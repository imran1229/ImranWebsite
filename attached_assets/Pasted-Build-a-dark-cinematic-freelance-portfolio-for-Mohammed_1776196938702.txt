Build a dark cinematic freelance portfolio for Mohammed Imran — graphic designer, video editor & motion designer.

DESIGN REFERENCE: Digital Flagship
Replicate this exact design language:

Near-black background (not pure black — deep charcoal like #0D0D0D)
White/off-white body text for maximum contrast
Amber/gold as the only accent colour (#F59E0B or warm gold) — used sparingly on 1–2 words per heading only, never decoratively
Zero clutter. Type IS the visual.


TYPOGRAPHY

Hero/display: Bold condensed uppercase sans-serif — use Barlow Condensed or Bebas Neue at massive scale (15–20vw). Each word stacked as its own typographic unit, dominating the full viewport
Section headings: Mixed case with italic or amber accent on select words (e.g. "Design that" moves) — not the full phrase, just the key word
Body: Clean humanist sans-serif, sentence case, noticeably smaller/lighter than headings
Nav labels: Small, tight letter-spacing, minimal

ANIMATIONS

Staggered per-word hero entrance on load
Infinite marquee ticker (two rows, opposite directions)
Scroll-triggered fade/slide-up on section entry
Project card hover: subtle scale + amber overlay reveal
Button hover: border fills amber left-to-right

RULES

No gradients. No illustrations. No stock photos in UI.
